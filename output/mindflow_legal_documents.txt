### 2. SERVICE DESCRIPTION

MindFlow is a mobile-first mental health support platform designed for college students. The Service includes:

**Core Features (Free Tier)**
- Daily mood tracking with pattern recognition
- Private journaling functionality
- Moderated peer support community forums
- Mental wellness content library (meditation, coping strategies)
- Crisis resources and safety planning tools

**Premium Features**
- On-demand access to licensed therapists via video and chat
