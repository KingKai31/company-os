### 2. SERVICE DESCRIPTION

InvoiceFlow is a cloud-based invoice automation platform that:

- Captures invoices via email, file upload, or API integration
- Uses artificial intelligence and optical character recognition (OCR) to extract invoice data
- Validates extracted data for accuracy and completeness
- Detects duplicate invoices and matches vendor information
- Routes approved invoices to Customer's accounting software (including QuickBooks, Xero, Wave, and NetSuite)
- Provides approval workflows, intelligent categorization, and real-time accounts payable (AP) reporting

**Service Limitations:**
- InvoiceFlow is designed for organizations with 10-100 employees processing 20-500 invoices monthly
- The Service processes invoices in English and common file formats (PDF, JPEG, PNG, TIFF)
- AI-powered extraction typically achieves 95-98% accuracy; Customer remains responsible for final validation
