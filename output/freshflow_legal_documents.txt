### 2. SERVICE DESCRIPTION

FreshFlow is a Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) platform designed to help restaurants and food service establishments manage inventory, track waste, forecast demand, and optimize ordering. Our Service includes:

- **Real-time Inventory Dashboard** - Visual tracking of stock levels via barcode, RFID, or computer vision
- **Waste Tracking & Analytics** - Automated categorization and cost quantification of food waste
- **Demand Forecasting** - Predictive analytics using menu data and point-of-sale (POS) history
- **Smart Ordering System** - Automated recommendations to optimize stock and reduce spoilage
- **Supplier Integration** - Automated purchasing connections with supplier systems
- **Sustainability & Compliance Reporting** - ESG metrics and regulatory reporting tools
- **Mobile & Web Access** - iOS/Android mobile app for staff; web dashboard for management
- **Third-Party Integrations** - Compatible with standard POS, accounting, and supplier platforms

The Service includes optional premium features using IoT sensors and hardware (detailed in your Subscription Plan).
