### 2. SERVICE DESCRIPTION

MindCampus provides a digital mental health and wellness platform that includes:

- Personalized mood tracking and AI-powered wellness insights
- Searchable directory of campus mental health resources and crisis support information
- Moderated peer support community by college campus
- Marketplace access to licensed telehealth counselors with college pricing
- Anonymous journaling and coping tool library
- Campus referral tracking for college counseling centers

The Service is available in two tiers:

**Free Tier:** Provides access to mood tracking, resource directory, peer support community, journaling tools, and basic referral tracking.

**Premium Tier ($4.99/month):** Provides unlimited access to licensed counselor sessions, advanced wellness analytics, priority support, and enhanced features.

**Important Limitation:** MindCampus is not a substitute for professional mental health treatment. If you are experiencing a mental health crisis, suicidal thoughts, or self-harm urges, please immediately contact emergency services (911 in the US) or a crisis hotline.
