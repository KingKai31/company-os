### 2. SERVICE DESCRIPTION

MindWell ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") provides a mobile-first mental health support application designed for college students. The Service includes:

- Daily mood tracking and trend analysis
- Mental health content library (meditations, exercises, articles)
- Moderated peer support community forums
- Crisis resource directory
- Journaling and reflection tools with AI-assisted analysis
- Preventive assessment tools (PHQ-9, GAD-7)
- Optional premium features including therapist matching and enhanced storage

**What MindWell Is NOT:** MindWell is not a replacement for professional mental health treatment, emergency services, or crisis intervention. The Service is a supplementary mental health support tool designed to complement, not substitute, care from licensed mental health professionals.
