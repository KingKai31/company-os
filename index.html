Launch your company with MindCampus

MindCampus is a mobile-first mental health platform for college students featuring: (1) AI-powered daily mood tracking and mental health assessments, (2) anonymous peer support chat and support groups organized by topic/challenge, (3) Direct access to campus counseling center scheduling and professional counselor messaging, (4) Crisis resource hub with campus-specific emergency contacts and crisis lines, (5) Personalized content feed with evidence-based coping strategies and wellness resources, (6) Campus resource directory (disability services, health center, support groups), (7) Referral/ambassador tools for students to invite peers, (8) Premium tier offering unlimited counselor access and personalized therapy plans. Focus on reducing stigma through peer support while ensuring safe escalation to professionals.
