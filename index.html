FitFlow

Empower busy professionals to build sustainable fitness habits through AI-personalized, time-efficient workouts that fit seamlessly into their demanding lives.

Built for: Busy professionals aged 25-45 with household income >$60K who work 40+ hours/week, value health but struggle with time constraints, use technology daily, and are willing to pay for convenient solutions. Primary segments: corporate employees in tech/finance/consulting, entrepreneurs, and remote workers in metropolitan areas. Pain points: lack of time for gym, inconsistent workout schedules, difficulty maintaining fitness habits, and information overload from generic fitness content.
